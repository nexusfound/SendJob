# DEC-009 — Pricing Model: $199 Setup + $97/Month Recurring

## Context

SendJob serves small trades businesses (HVAC, plumbing, pool, cleaning, lawn). Pricing must be accessible for owner-operators while reflecting the automation value delivered. Beta clients were brought on at a discounted rate to build early traction.

## Decision

Standard pricing: $199 one-time setup fee + $97/month recurring. Beta clients are permanently locked at $97/month (no setup fee).

## Impact

- Stripe live payment links are active for this pricing — do not regenerate without verifying webhook endpoints
- Beta client rate is permanent; billing logic must never overwrite their rate
- Pricing is the anchor for all GTM and Playbook demo conversion flows
