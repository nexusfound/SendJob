# DEC-007 — Website Builds as a Service Ruled Out

## Context

During market evaluation, website builds as a service were considered as a potential revenue stream. Analysis showed low margins (commodity market) with high time investment per client. The Nexus Playbook approach (standardized industry-specific templates) was validated as the correct path instead.

## Decision

Website builds as a custom service are explicitly out of scope for SendJob / Nexus Found. The Playbook provides templated, self-deploying demo sites — not custom development.

## Impact

- Scope boundary is firm: automation and Playbook deployment, not custom website work
- Prevents scope creep into low-margin services during Discovery or delivery
- Playbook plays are templatized from AC demo — fast to deploy, not custom
