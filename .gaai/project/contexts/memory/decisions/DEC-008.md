# DEC-008 — GoHighLevel, Lovable, and WordPress Evaluated and Rejected

## Context

Several all-in-one platforms and CMS tools were evaluated as potential replacements or complements to the n8n + Supabase + Netlify stack.

## Decision

GoHighLevel, Lovable, and WordPress were evaluated and not adopted. The current stack (n8n + Supabase + Netlify) is the confirmed technical direction.

## Impact

- No future discovery should revisit these without new evidence of changed constraints
- These platforms were rejected for reasons of cost, lock-in, or capability mismatch
- Events/bartending vertical also ruled out (CheckCherry dominates at $29/month — too competitive)
- Current stack gives full control, lower total cost, and better automation flexibility
