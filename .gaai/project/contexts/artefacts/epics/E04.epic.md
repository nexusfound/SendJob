# Epic E04: Job Complete + End-to-End Validation

## Purpose

The technician can mark a job complete from their mobile dashboard, triggering an automatic SMS to the customer with a payment link. The full job lifecycle is validated end-to-end before the first pilot client goes live.

---

## Scope

- Job Complete button on tech dashboard (status transition: On Site → Complete)
- N8N workflow: customer SMS with payment link triggered on job completion
- E2E test script covering the full 13-step job lifecycle

---

## Out of Scope

- Time-based token expiry (no sprint assigned — deferred from E03S08)
- "Text JOBS" self-serve token renewal flow (deferred from E03S08)
- In-app payment processing — clients provide their own payment links via `clients.payment_link`
- Automated E2E test tooling (Playwright, etc.)

---

## Stories

- E04S01: Tech dashboard — Job Complete button (shown when status = On Site)
- E04S02: N8N: Job Complete → customer SMS with payment link + sentinel
- E04S03: E2E test script — full 13-step manual checklist

---

## Success Metrics

- Tech can mark a job complete from their mobile dashboard in one tap
- Customer receives SMS with payment link within 30 seconds of job marked complete
- Full job lifecycle runs without manual intervention from form submission through completion
- E2E test passes in a single run on real devices before Sprint 5 begins

---

## Dependencies

- E03 (Tech Flow) — all stories complete
